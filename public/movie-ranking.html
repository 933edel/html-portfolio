The Best Movies According to Siddharth

Siddharth's Top 3 Movies to Watch

The wolf of wall street

An amazing story based on the real life businessman Jordan Belfort. The movie has some beautiful life lessons interlaced with brilliant comedy and marvellous performances.

Man of Steel

A great movie about none other than the Superman. The last son of Krypton has a lot to learn from. The score is fantastic and the production and performances kick ass!

The Great Gatsby

Based on the legendary novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, this one is a joy to watch. The representation of New York in the late 1900s is amazing and the visual metaphors are a cherry on the cake.
